 Who is the pre-assessment for?

It is for those who :

  • Don’t know what immigration process to follow
  • Don’t have a particular urgent situation or pending immigration procedure with immigration

It is not for those who :

  • You have any type of emergency, for example, you or your family have a deportation order
  • You are in the United States and wish to enter Canada as a refugee and/or have already left your country en route to Canada seeking protection
  • You are familiar with the immigration process
Determine Your Odds To Migrate To Canada

By proceeding with this option, the CI Canada team will assess your potential to migrate to Canada.

Step 1 of 4
Register with cI canada
  • Register through the payment form below.
  • Upon completion of payment, we will contact you via email to ask you several questions.
Step 2 of 4
submit information

Based on the information you provide us, we will determine if you have potential to immigrate to Canada.

Step 3 of 4
appointment scheduling
  • If you are determined to have potential, you will receive an email to schedule a completely free appointment with CI Canada.
  • If you are determined to not be a good candidate at this time, we will notify you via email.
Step 4 of 4
take action

After your free appointment with CI Canada, you will be able to continue the process and decide to take an Express Assessment to confirm your project.

This is not an extensive consultation

This Pre-Assessment is a first analysis, and is not meant to be an extensive assessment that will provide you with a detailed plan of action. For more comprehensive assessments and personalized action plans, please seek our 30 minute or 1 hour paid consultations.
